💡 Interview Tips
01

Know Your Basics Cold

Interviewers always test fundamentals — Python, SQL, Statistics, and ML concepts. Revise them daily even if you feel confident.

02

Build Real Projects

Having 2–3 strong projects on GitHub is more impressive than 10 certificates. Always be ready to explain every line of your code.

03

Practice on Kaggle & LeetCode

For Data roles — solve Kaggle competitions. For developer roles — solve LeetCode easy/medium problems regularly.

04

Communicate Your Thinking

During interviews, think out loud. Interviewers care about how you approach a problem, not just the final answer.

05

Prepare Your STAR Stories

For HR rounds — prepare 3–4 stories using STAR format (Situation, Task, Action, Result) from your project experiences.

06

Research the Company

Before every interview, spend 30 mins researching the company — their products, tech stack, and recent news.

  • Keep it to 1 page for freshers

  • Post about your projects regularly

  • Write a strong headline — not just "Student"

  • LinkedIn Jobs — best for tech roles
  • Internshala — best for internships
  • Naukri.com — popular for Indian market
  • AngelList — great for startups
  • Company career pages directly

⏰ Timeline for Freshers

  • 3 months before — update resume & LinkedIn
